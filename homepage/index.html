Welcome to Local Wildlife Tracker

Wildlife Tracker is designed to empower communities to observe, protect and, support local animal populations and their habitats. By providing tools to report wildlife sightings, track species health and their changes in animal behavior or habitat conditions, our platform encourages conservation and engagement with the natural world.

our goal is to increase awareness of local biodiversity, promote and educaate the public about the vital role animals have in maintaining a balanced ecosystem. whether you're a student, volunteer, or concerned neighbor, Wildlife Tracker offers the knowledge and support needed to make informed decisions that help protect and preserve local wildlife.

Threats to Wildlife

Wildlife face numerous challenges. Habitat loss from construction, pollution, and traffic reduces the space animals need to feed, breed, and thrive.

Pollution from traffic, waste, and industrial activities harms local wildlife by contaminating the air they breate, the water they drink and the habitats they are dependant on.

Human activities such as cutting hedgerows, removing trees, or encroaching on green spaces can fragment habitats, making it harder for wildlife to survive.

Why Animal Wildlife Matters

Animals are essential to healthy ecosystems. They can help control pests, pollinate plants, disperse seeds and maintain the balance of nature.

Protecting animal populations ensures biodiversity, strenghtens resilience against environmental changes, and keeps urban and rural ecosystems functioning properly. Every species, no matter how small, plays a critical role this makes it crucial for the communities to raise awareness as animals play a vital role to sustain a healthy ecosystem.

Wildlife in Our Communities

Even in urban areas like London, diverse animal species thrive in parks, gardens and green spaces in general. Foxes, hedgehogs,birds, insects are indicatirs if healthy ecosystems. By observing wildlife, communities can look at areas that need protection, help promote coexistence between animals and humans, this way it develops a healthy ecosystem in the future.
