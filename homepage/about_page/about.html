About Us

Meet the team behind Wildlife Tracker and learn about our mission.

Our Mission

15

Supporting UN SDG 15: Life on Land

Wildlife Tracker was created as part of a university group project to raise awareness about biodiversity conservation. Our platform aligns with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 15, which focuses on protecting terrestrial ecosystems and halting biodiversity loss.

We believe that by connecting people with their local wildlife, we can inspire meaningful action to protect the natural world around us.

Meet the Team

We are Group B15 - a team of Brunel University students passionate about wildlife conservation.
