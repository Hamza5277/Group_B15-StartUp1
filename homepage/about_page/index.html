About Wildlife Tracker

Empowering communities to observe, protect, and support local wildlife populations through innovative tracking and reporting technology.

Meet the Team

What We're Building

Wildlife Tracker is designed to empower communities to observe, protect, and support local animal populations and their habitats. By providing tools to report wildlife sightings, track species health and their changes, to submit threats or habitat conditions, our platform encourages communities and engagement with the natural world.

Our goal is to increase awareness of local biodiversity, promote and educate the public about the vital role animals have in maintaining a balanced ecosystem across sectors, education, conservation, and community outreach.

UN SDG 15: Life on Land

This project directly supports UN Sustainable Development Goal 15, which aims to protect, restore, and promote sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems, sustainably manage forests, combat desertification, halt and reverse land degradation, and halt biodiversity loss.

Biodiversity Conservation
