What We're Building

A comprehensive platform designed to connect communities with their local ecosystems through wildlife observation and data-driven conservation.

The Project

Wildlife Tracker is designed to empower communities to observe, protect, and support local animal populations and their habitats. By providing tools to report wildlife sightings, track species health and their changes, to submit threats or habitat conditions, our platform encourages communities and engagement with the natural world.

Our goal is to increase awareness of local biodiversity, promote and educate the public about the vital role animals have in maintaining a balanced ecosystem across sectors, education, conservation, and community outreach. Wildlife Tracker offers the knowledge and support needed to make informed decisions that help protect and preserve local wildlife.

UN SDG Alignment

This project directly supports UN Sustainable Development Goal 15: Life on Land, which aims to protect, restore, and promote sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems, sustainably manage forests, combat desertification, halt and reverse land degradation, and halt biodiversity loss.

By enabling citizens to document and report wildlife sightings, we contribute to building a comprehensive database that can inform conservation efforts, identify threatened species, and track the health of local ecosystems over time.
